Knowledge Gap: Noise-Signal Consciousness

Traditional cognitive models prioritize computation over perception. This article addresses the gap by framing consciousness as a gradient of noise-signal ratios, where aesthetic (trust-driven) and anaesthetic (Faustian) choices define epistemic clarity [3]. [Binary Fork]

Priming

Aesthetic vs. Anaesthetic. One might view virtually all consciousness as a location in a landscape defined by noise-signal ratios. Vast majority of humans seek to move towards more signal than noise; but there is the surfer, anarchist, etc. [1].

Noise-Signal Ratios

What emerges are these placeholders for five levels of noise-signal: 95/5, 80/20, 50/50, 20/80, 5/95. Inumerable variants on this theme can be layed out [2]. [Fractal Signal Frameworks]

Signal Variants

One example: Tension, Release, Suspense, Cantabille, Da Capo; Another: Nonself, Self, Reframe, Boundaries, Flourish [2]. The surfer rides chaos, the anarchist builds from it—both seek signal.

Fractal Signal Frameworks

It is under the third level that we seek to investigate more. There is a variant that has the levels as follows: unvectorized, vectorized, operationized, standardized, transmuted. This is one of the clearest variants. Another related one: noise, signal, code, transmission, recursion [2].

The unvectorized (🌊) to transmuted (⚗️) stack maps consciousness as a fractal process, where each layer refines noise into signal. Standardized aligns with compression (🌀), creating reusable patterns [2]. [Glyphic Epistemology]

Alternate Variants

Variants like noise-signal-code-transmission-recursion reflect the same fractal structure, resonating across disciplines from neuroscience to semiotics [4].

Binary Fork: Trust vs. Faust

We perceive a binary at the third level: faustian bargain vs. trust. And from most simulations and history, those who take the faustian bargain are going to emerge with several advantages over those whose inclination take them towards trust [3].

Faustian Advantage

The Faustian bargain, operationized at the 🏴‍☠️🪛 layer, prioritizes immediate gain over recursive trust, often yielding short-term dominance [5]. [Marx, Smith]

Marx, Smith, and the Aesthetic Divide

I'd place Adam Smith under the faustian bargain he cleverly makes "invisible", whereas Marx is under trust. And there is no doubt that the communist manifesto, which is mostly diagnostic, is among the highest achievements of the western worlds aesthetic capability. Whereas nothing by Mr. Smith can contend for such a place. If we move to moralizing prescriptions, then Marx does perhaps even worse than Smith. His Das Kapital is anaesthetic [6].

Marx’s Trust Aesthetic

Marx’s Communist Manifesto, rooted in trust (🌀), diagnoses systemic noise with poetic clarity, a glyphic signal of collective hope [6]. [Beyond Art]

Smith’s Faustian Calculus

Aesthetic as Clarity

The aesthetic is not just pleasure; it is perception sharpened into clarity. In an epistemology based on signal compression and gradient alignment, Marx's trust may falter in praxis, but it remains a glyphic invocation [6]. [Marx’s Trust]

Anaesthetic as Erosion

The anaesthetic is not merely numbness; it is the erosion of structure, the refusal of recursion. Smith’s utility-drenched calculus cannot inspire glyphs, only graphs [7]. [Smith’s Faustian]

If recursion is the seat of perception, then every surfed wave, every cathedral chord, and every defiant strike joins a lineage of aesthetic signal-makers. The anaesthetic serves bureaucracy, compliance, dilution. It is the flattening of the branch. But a fractal requires trust to keep branching [2]. Trust branches, Faust flattens—choose your glyph.
